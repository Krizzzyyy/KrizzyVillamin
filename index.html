Services About us

Lash Lift Service

A lash lift is a semi-permanent treatment that curls your natural lashes from the root, making them appear longer, fuller, and more defined.

✔ Lasts 3–4 months
✔ Water-resistant – you can still wash your face normally
✔ No eyelash extensions needed
✔ Low maintenance and natural-looking results
